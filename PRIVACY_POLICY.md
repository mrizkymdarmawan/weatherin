# Privacy Policy for weatherin

Last updated: March 2026

weatherin does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

The app uses your device location only to display local weather.
This data is sent directly to Open-Meteo (open-meteo.com) to
fetch weather information and is never stored or shared.

No account is required. No analytics. No tracking. No ads.

If you have any questions, contact: your@email.com
